Laws & Guardians

Maintaining a fair and epic experience for every pioneer in the Core realm.

gavel SERVER RULES

v2.4 Final
01

NO GRIEFING

Do not destroy, alter, or damage player-made structures without explicit permission from the owner. Respect the effort of your fellow pioneers.

02

RESPECT ALL PLAYERS

Harassment, hate speech, sexism, and toxicity are strictly prohibited. Core Network is an inclusive space. Keep the banter friendly and constructive in all chat channels.

03

NO CHEATING OR EXPLOITS

The use of hacked clients, X-ray packs, auto-clickers, or exploiting server bugs for an unfair advantage will result in an immediate and permanent ban. Play fair, play long.

04

SPAM & ADVERTISING

Do not spam the global chat with repetitive messages or advertise other servers. Community links (Twitch/YouTube) are permitted only in designated channels.

05

COMMON SENSE

Not every rule is explicitly written. Use your judgment. If an action feels like it harms the community, it likely isn't allowed.

06

STAFF DIRECTIONS

Always follow the instructions provided by Core Staff. If you feel a decision is unfair, use the proper appeal channels on our Discord rather than arguing in public chat.
